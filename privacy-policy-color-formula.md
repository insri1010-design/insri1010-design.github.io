# Privacy Policy for Color Formula

**Last updated: 2026-04-18**

## 1. Overview
Color Formula ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information Collection and Use
While we do not require you to provide personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or phone number, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

### A. Advertising (Google AdMob)
To provide free content, our app includes advertisements served by Google AdMob. For this purpose, we collect and share the following data:
* **Device or other IDs:** Such as the Google Advertising ID (AD ID). This is used for personalized advertising, frequency capping, and fraud prevention.
* **App Activity:** Information about how you interact with ads within the app.

### B. In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)
If you purchase the "Premium Pass," all transactions are processed through the Google Play Billing system. We do not store or have access to your credit card or financial information. Google provides us with a transaction receipt to verify your purchase and grant access to premium features.

### C. Analytics and Performance
We collect anonymous data regarding app performance and crashes to improve the user experience.
* **Crash Logs & Diagnostics:** To identify and fix technical issues.

## 3. Third-Party Service Providers
Our app uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:
* **Google Play Services:** [https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
* **AdMob:** [https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

## 4. Data Security and Encryption
We care about the security of your data. All data collected (such as Advertising IDs and Performance data) is **encrypted in transit** using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS).

## 5. Children’s Privacy
Our game is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
